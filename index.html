What's up?! I'm Sev.

I am web designer and developer who thrives on visual storytelling and great user experience. When I'm not making websites or learning new skills, I can be found making music and shooting and editing videos.

I was born and raised in Hawaii on the beautiful island of Maui. I started playing music at a young age and eventually attended a private arts college in Seattle for composition and music theory.

To me creating a website is like creating a song. Just like a musician needs to know music theory, a web designer and developer needs to know the languages of the web and the basics of design.

The creative possibilities are infinte even when given a set of rules to work within. Equipped with the knowledge, I can create a variety of experiences, each evoking their own emotions and intended tone.
